## What is Miona?
Miona is a versatile and highly customizable Discord bot designed to enhance your server with a wide range of powerful features. Its core strength lies in the ability to customize nearly every aspect, allowing you to tailor the bot’s behavior to fit your community perfectly. Here’s a breakdown of what Miona offers:

- **🖼️ AutoPFP**: Automatically sends random profile pictures in a channel to engage your community. - 
- **🔗 AutoMatch**: Automatically sends random matching profile pictures in a channel.
- **😂 Auto Meme**: Shares trending memes directly in your server to keep the fun going.
- **👋 Customizable Greeting System**: Create personalized welcome, leave, and boost messages to give your server a unique touch.
- **🎉 Giveaways System**: Host and manage giveaways effortlessly with fully customizable options.
- **🎫 Customizable Tickets System**: Set up a ticketing system for support and queries that suits your needs, with adjustable templates and categories.
- **🏷️ Auto Roles**: Automatically assign roles to new members, streamlining onboarding and organization.
- **📈 Invites Tracker**: Track server invites to see who’s bringing in new members.
- **🛡️ Moderation Tools**: Utilize advanced moderation features to keep your server safe and well-managed.
- **🌟 Vanity Roles**: Allow members to earn or choose special roles for themselves, adding a personalized flair.
- **📊 Custom Counters**: Display server stats such as online members, offline members, and more with fully customizable counters.
- and much more.

Miona stands out by offering the highest level of customization, enabling you to fine-tune every feature according to your community's needs.
